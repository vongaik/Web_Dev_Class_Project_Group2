Interested in Getting Involved on Marietta?

Why it's important to get involved:

Image

Organizations at Kennesaw State University actively participate in and contribute to a vibrant university community. Approximately 300 Registered Student Organizations (RSOs) at KSU provide transformative experiences and opportunities for students to grow as individuals, leaders, and community members.

Research has shown that students who are involved on campus are more likely to be successful and to gain more from their time in college (Astin, 1984). Accordingly, student organizations provide opportunities to gain valuable experience in the practical aspects of leadership, communication, risk management, and teamwork. As such, student engagement in clubs and organizations are an important component to campus life at KSU that provide an outlet for the student voice to be heard and a vehicle for students to have a substantial impact on campus.

Sign Up for Activities

All Registered Student Organizations (RSOs) can be found in Owl Life. Students can search for organizations by following these steps:

  1. Visit the on the "Organizations" tab on Owl Life.
  2. Browse by organization name or categories (found on the left-hand side of the screen).
  3. Click on an organization to visit their specific Owl Life page. Learn about the purposes of each student organization and how to become a member.
  4. To let an organization know you are interested in joining, click the blue "Join" button in the top right corner of the RSO's page if they have one. You can also email their RSO email address or any of the officers.

Registering an Organization

The Department of Student Activities' staff is excited that you are ready and willing to provide another avenue for KSU students to get involved and we are here to support you along the way.
Step 1: Develop a Mission and Purpose
Search the Organization Directory in Owl Life to make sure that your new student organization does not have a similar purpose as an existing KSU organization.
Step 2: Find Members
Find 6 members and record their names, KSU e-mail addresses, and net-IDs to submit in the “New Student Organization Registration” form (step 4). Delegate key leadership roles.
Step 3: Find an On-Campus Advisor
Find a full-time employee of KSU that would like to serve as the organization's advisor (faculty or staff). The Advisor must be listed on the Owl Life roster and accept the Terms and Conditions.
Step 4: Create a Constitution
Create a constitution for the organization with the required KSU articles and positions. See the Sample Constitution for assistance!
Step 5: Online Officer Training
Log on to CampusTraining with your Net-ID and password. Enroll in the course: "Student Organization Training AY 2022-2023." Complete all modules and download the completion certificate.
Step 6: Owl Life Registration Form
Complete the “New Student Organization Registration” form in its entirety. Go to owllife.kennesaw.edu/register and then click the blue “Register a New Organization” button to begin the form.
Step 7: Getting Started Meeting
After completion of these steps a Student Activities staff member will contact the officers & advisor to schedule a “Getting Started” meeting to discuss ways the organization be successful.
